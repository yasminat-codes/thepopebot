# Detection Signatures — Every AI Tell

**Complete catalog of AI fingerprints: what they look like, how to detect them, and how severe each is.**

---

## Severity Levels

| Level | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 🔴 Critical | Almost never appears in human writing. Extremely reliable AI indicator. |
| 🟠 High | Rarely appears in human writing at this frequency. Strong AI indicator. |
| 🟡 Medium | Appears in human writing but AI overuses it. Elevated frequency = AI. |
| 🟢 Low | Could be either. Requires context to determine. |

## Category 2: Sentence Structure Tells

### 🔴 Critical Structure Signatures

| Signature | How to Detect |
|-----------|--------------|
| **The Perfect Triple** | Sentences with exactly 3 parallel items appearing >4x per 500 words |
| **Length Uniformity** | All sentences 15-25 words with variance under 5 words |
| **No Contractions** | 0 contractions in 500+ words of conversational writing |
| **Zero Fragments** | Every sentence grammatically complete — no fragments at all |
| **Every Paragraph 3-4 Sentences** | Check paragraph counts — if all 3 or 4, it's AI |

### 🟠 High Structure Signatures

| Signature | How to Detect |
|-----------|--------------|
| **Passive Flood** | >4 passive constructions per 500 words |
| **Transition Sequence** | "Furthermore...Additionally...Moreover" or any 2+ in sequence |
| **Subject-Start Monotony** | >8/10 sentences starting with subject |
| **Perfect Parallel Structure** | >5 instances per 500 words of lists with identical grammatical pattern |
| **No Run-ons** | All sentences cleanly punctuated — no informal run-ons |
| **Semicolon Overuse** | >3 semicolons per 500 words in casual/conversational writing |

### 🟡 Medium Structure Signatures

| Signature | Threshold |
|-----------|----------|
| Transition openers | >4 per 500 words |
| Hedge constructions | >5 per 500 words |
| Triple lists | >3 per 500 words |
| Passive constructions | >2 per 500 words |

## Category 3: Formatting Tells

### 🔴 Critical Formatting Signatures

| Signature | Description |
|-----------|------------|
| **Header Addiction** | Headers appearing in content under 1,000 words that isn't documentation |
| **Bullet Everything** | More than 30% of content in bullet lists |
| **Nested Bullets** | Sub-bullets under sub-bullets |
| **"Key Takeaways" Section** | Explicit recap section at end of short content |
| **Introduction/Conclusion Headers** | In content under 1,500 words |
| **TOC on Short Content** | Table of contents on anything under 2,000 words |

### 🟠 High Formatting Signatures

| Signature | Description |
|-----------|------------|
| **Random Bold** | More than 10% of prose bolded |
| **Numbered Non-Sequences** | Numbered list where items have no natural order |
| **"In This Article" Opener** | Article opening with what it will cover |
| **Bullet Then Explain Each** | Bullet list immediately followed by paragraph explaining each bullet |
| **3-Section Default** | Every response: intro paragraph + bullet list + conclusion paragraph |

## Category 4: Tone Tells

### 🔴 Critical Tone Signatures

| Signature | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| **Compliment Opener** | "Great question!", "Excellent point!", "That's a wonderful observation!" |
| **Hope-This-Helps Closer** | "I hope this helps!", "I hope this provides clarity!" |
| **Everything-Is-Complex Escape** | "This is a nuanced topic with many perspectives..." without follow-through |
| **Performative Balance** | "Both perspectives have merit" with no actual position taken |
| **The Empathy Script** | "I understand how frustrating this must be for you" |

### 🟠 High Tone Signatures

| Signature | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| **Hollow Opener** | "In today's fast-paced world..." "In today's digital landscape..." |
| **The Setup Flood** | >3 "It's important to..." / "It's worth noting..." per 500 words |
| **Vague Expert Appeal** | "Experts agree..." / "Research shows..." without specifics |
| **Circular Non-Answer** | Restates the question as the answer |
| **Corporate Register Leak** | Formal corporate language in casual context |

## Category 5: Punctuation Tells

### 🟠 High Punctuation Signatures

| Signature | Detection |
|-----------|----------|
| **Em-Dash Spray** | More than 3 em-dashes (`—`) per 500 words in non-formal writing |
| **Semicolon Abuse** | >3 semicolons per 500 words |
| **Comma-Then-And** | "X, and Y, and Z" pattern (AI connects everything) |
| **Colon Before Lists Always** | Every list preceded by a colon, even mid-sentence |

## Category 6: Content Tells

### 🟠 High Content Signatures

| Signature | Description |
|-----------|------------|
| **The Perfect Answer** | Covers exactly the question, nothing more, nothing less — no tangents |
| **Uniform Detail Level** | Every sub-point gets exactly the same depth of explanation |
| **No Strong Opinion** | 500+ words on a debatable topic with no clear position |
| **Historical Context Injection** | Unnecessary brief history added to every topic |
| **"There Are X Types" Pattern** | Classifying everything into neat numbered categories |
| **The 3-Point Conclusion** | Every piece ends: (1) summary, (2) implication, (3) next steps |

## Quick Scan Checklist

For rapid AI detection, look for:

**Instant tells (one of these = probably AI):**
- [ ] "delve into" appears
- [ ] "utilize" appears where "use" works
- [ ] "in today's X landscape" opener
- [ ] Headers on a 400-word piece
- [ ] "Furthermore" + "Additionally" + "Moreover" in same document
- [ ] "Great question!" opener
- [ ] "Key Takeaways" / "In Conclusion" section
- [ ] Zero contractions in conversational writing
- [ ] Every paragraph exactly 3-4 sentences

**Pattern tells (two or more = AI):**
- [ ] >3 em-dashes in 500 words
- [ ] >4 passive constructions in 500 words
- [ ] Perfect 15-25 word sentence uniformity
- [ ] >4 transition openers (Furthermore/Additionally etc.)
- [ ] >5 hedge phrases in 500 words
- [ ] >3 "important to note" / "worth noting" setups
- [ ] More bullets than prose sentences
