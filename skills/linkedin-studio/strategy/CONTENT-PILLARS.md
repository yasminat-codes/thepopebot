## Pillar Definitions (Yasmine-Specific)

### Thought Leadership (35%)

**What it means for Yasmine:** Original takes on AI adoption, the gap between AI hype and reality, what she sees building systems for small businesses. Bold opinions backed by building experience.

| Attribute | Guidance |
|---|---|
| Hook types | Contrarian, stat-backed, bold claim |
| Story bank tags | `industry-observation`, `hype-vs-reality`, `enterprise-vs-small-biz` |
| Example angles | "AI agents will replace most SaaS dashboards", "Why 80% of AI projects fail in week 1", "The AI tool everyone recommends that I'd never use" |
| CTA style | Soft — "What's your take?", "Agree or disagree?" |
| Voice | Opinionated, direct, nurse-triage framing ("Let me diagnose this") |

### Education (25%)

**What it means for Yasmine:** Practical how-to content that teaches small business owners something they can do today. Frameworks, tool breakdowns, step-by-step guides. The reader walks away knowing how to do something.

| Attribute | Guidance |
|---|---|
| Hook types | Question hook, "X steps to Y", "The framework I use for..." |
| Story bank tags | `building-smarterflo`, `client-work`, `nurse-skills-transfer` |
| Example angles | "The 3-question AI triage for your business", "How I built a 12-agent team (simplified)", "What to ask before hiring an AI consultant" |
| CTA style | Resource — "DM me 'FRAMEWORK' for the template" |
| Voice | Teacher, practical, step-by-step, nursing metaphors (charting, vitals) |

### Social Proof (25%)

**What it means for Yasmine:** Evidence that her methods work. Client results, before/after transformations, project retrospectives. Real numbers, real outcomes.

| Attribute | Guidance |
|---|---|
| Hook types | Story hook (first-person narrative), stat hook (specific result) |
| Story bank tags | `client-win`, `before-after`, `building-smarterflo` |
| Example angles | "A client went from 6hr manual reviews to 20min", "What happened when I replaced 3 tools with 1 agent", "The project that almost failed (and what saved it)" |
| CTA style | Direct — "Want similar results? DM me 'CONSULT'" |
| Voice | Storyteller, specific, receipts-driven |

### CTA / Conversion (15%)

**What it means for Yasmine:** Direct lead generation. Limited spots, specific offers, clear action. The purpose is to move followers into conversations.

| Attribute | Guidance |
|---|---|
| Hook types | Question hook, bold claim, scarcity |
| Story bank tags | any — CTA posts can reference any story |
| Example angles | "Opening 3 AI audit spots this month", "Free AI readiness check for businesses under 50 people", "DM me your biggest manual workflow" |
| CTA style | Hard — "DM me 'AI AUDIT'", "Book a call: [link]" |
| Voice | Direct, confident, no hedging |

## Balancing Rules

1. **No more than 2 consecutive posts from the same pillar.** If the last two were TL, the next must be different.
2. **CTA posts never back-to-back.** Always buffer with at least one non-CTA post.
3. **Weekly distribution check.** At each calendar planning session, verify the upcoming week hits at least 3 of 4 pillars.
4. **Monthly rebalancing.** If any pillar drifts more than 10% from target over a calendar month, flag in analytics.
5. **Social Proof posts require real data.** Never fabricate or exaggerate metrics. Use anonymized client data when NDA applies.
