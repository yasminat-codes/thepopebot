# AI Vocabulary — Complete Banned Words & Replacements

**The master list of AI vocabulary tells with contextual replacements. Research-backed, sourced from AI detector analysis and pattern frequency studies.**

---

## How to Use This List

- Replace flagged words with the suggested alternatives
- Context matters — choose the replacement that fits the sentence, not the first option
- Some words are banned only as verbs (e.g., "leverage") but fine as nouns
- When in doubt, cut the word entirely and restructure the sentence
- Tier 1 = replace every time. Tier 2 = replace in non-technical contexts. Tier 3 = frequency check.

---

## Tier 1: Hard Bans (Replace Every Time)

These words are almost exclusively AI tells. Real humans use them rarely if ever in prose.

AI consistently opens sentences and paragraphs with these. "Furthermore" is used 15x more often by AI than humans.

| Opener | Fix |
|--------|-----|

## Tier 4: The LinkedIn AI Pattern (Platform-Specific)

These phrases are almost exclusive to AI-generated LinkedIn content.

| Pattern | What It Is | Fix |
|---------|-----------|-----|
| `I'm thrilled to announce` | announcement opener | "We just launched X." or just announce |
| `I'm excited to share` | share opener | Just share it |
| `Humbled and honored` | achievement opener | Just describe the achievement |
| `I had the privilege of` | experience opener | "I [did the thing]" |
| `Here's what I learned:` (bullet list follows) | "lessons" format | Write the lesson in prose |
| `Here's the thing:` | buildup before obvious point | Cut and say the thing |
| `Here's what most people miss:` | AI contrarian hook | Cut or write the observation directly |
| `Unpopular opinion:` | manufactured controversy | Just state the opinion |
| `Hot take:` | same | |
| `If you're not doing X, you're missing out` | FOMO opener | State the benefit directly |
| `X years ago, I [did Y]. Today, [contrast]` | transformation narrative | Fine if genuine, AI-sounding if not |
| `This is the most important thing I've learned` | superlative setup | Cut the superlative, make the point |
| `What most people don't realize` | manufactured insight | Just make the point |
| `Drop a 🔥 in the comments if` | engagement bait | Cut entirely |
| `What do you think? Comment below!` | hollow CTA | Cut or ask specific question |
| `Repost if this resonated` | viral bait | Cut |
| `Follow me for more content like this` | growth beg | Cut |
| `Agree? Disagree? Let me know!` | hollow engagement | Cut or ask what you actually want to know |
