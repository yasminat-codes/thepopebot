## Enforcement Rules

1. **ALL phrases are HIGH severity** — every match triggers mandatory replacement
2. **Every replacement is concrete** — no "rephrase", "be specific", or "specific alternative"
3. **[DELETE] means delete the phrase entirely** — do not replace with another filler
4. **Scan output twice** — if any blocklist phrase remains after first pass, flag and replace manually
5. **Never replace a blocklist phrase with another blocklist phrase** — check the replacement against this list
6. **Context determines which replacement to use** — when multiple options exist, pick the one that reads most naturally in the sentence
7. **Contractions over formality** — always prefer "don't" over "do not", "can't" over "cannot" etc.
8. **When in doubt: shorter, plainer, more direct**
9. **Zero means zero** — a post that passes humanization should contain NONE of these phrases
