# Offer Validation Rubric

5-dimension quality rubric. Every offer generated by solution-offer-builder must be scored before output is assembled. Run this after Step 10 (cold copy) and before Step 11 (output contract assembly).

---

## Dimensions

Score each dimension 0–2. Max total: 10.

### Dimension 1: Specificity (0–2)

- **0** — Generic: "save time and money" language, no concrete numbers
- **1** — Semi-specific: mentions the pain category but no quantification
- **2** — Highly specific: references exact time/cost/process from evidence_quotes, uses prospect's own language

### Dimension 2: Differentiation (0–2)

- **0** — Could be any agency: no differentiation from competitors mentioned
- **1** — Acknowledges competitors but doesn't clearly out-position them
- **2** — Explicitly positions against competitor weakness in competitor_map: "unlike X which [complaint], this [advantage]"

### Dimension 3: Reddit Language Match (0–2)

- **0** — Uses AI-sounding language: "leverage synergies", "optimize workflows", "drive ROI"
- **1** — Plain English but not using the exact Reddit-voiced phrases
- **2** — Uses verbatim or near-verbatim language from evidence_quotes in the offer angle or cold copy

### Dimension 4: Outcome Clarity (0–2)

- **0** — Outcome is vague: "helps with", "improves", "supports"
- **1** — Has an outcome but it's not measurable (e.g. "removes manual work")
- **2** — Has 2+ measurable outcomes with specifics (e.g. "eliminate 8 hours/week of CRM updates" + "90% fewer missed follow-ups")

### Dimension 5: CTA Friction (0–2)

- **0** — High friction: "Sign up now", "Schedule a demo", "Buy now" as first ask
- **1** — Medium friction: "Let's jump on a call" without qualification or time estimate
- **2** — Low friction: specific, time-bound, zero-commitment ask ("10-minute walkthrough this week" / "I'll audit your current setup for free")

---

## Scoring Gate

| Score | Action |
|-------|--------|
| ≥ 7 | Offer passes. Include in output with `offer_validation_score: N` |
| 5–6 | Borderline. Include with `offer_validation_score: N, validation_warning: "borderline — review before use"` |
| < 5 | Fail. Do NOT include. Attempt one regeneration targeting failed dimensions. If still < 5: set `failed_validation: true`, include reasoning. |

---

## Regeneration Instructions by Dimension Failure

Apply these instructions when an offer scores < 5. Target only the failing dimensions.

| Dimension failed | Regeneration instruction |
|-----------------|--------------------------|
| Specificity | "Add exact figures from evidence_quotes. Replace 'save time' with the specific hours/tasks named in the quote." |
| Differentiation | "Add a vs_competitor line that directly references the complaint_themes in competitor_map." |
| Reddit Language | "Replace all offer language with words/phrases directly from the evidence_quotes. Copy verbatim where possible." |
| Outcome Clarity | "State 2 specific outcomes with numbers: hours saved, tasks eliminated, percentage improvement, revenue gained." |
| CTA Friction | "Change CTA to a specific, time-bound, zero-commitment ask. Use '10-minute X' or 'free Y' format." |

## Worked Example

### Failing Offer (Score: 4)

**Offer angle:** "We help SaaS companies improve their workflows and save time on operations."

**Cold copy CTA:** "Sign up today for a free demo."

**Scoring:**

| Dimension | Score | Reason |
|-----------|-------|--------|
| Specificity | 0 | "save time" — no numbers, no evidence_quotes language |
| Differentiation | 1 | Mentions SaaS but no competitor positioning |
| Reddit Language | 0 | "improve their workflows" — AI-sounding, not Reddit-voiced |
| Outcome Clarity | 1 | "save time on operations" — not measurable |
| CTA Friction | 2 | "free demo" is low commitment, but "Sign up" framing is high friction |

**Total: 4 — FAILS validation.**

**Regeneration instructions applied:**
- Specificity: Add exact figures from evidence_quotes ("I spend 2 hours a day just updating HubSpot")
- Reddit Language: Replace with verbatim quote language
- Outcome Clarity: State 2 specific outcomes with numbers

### Passing Offer After Regeneration (Score: 8)

**Offer angle:** "Bootstrapped SaaS founders who are spending 2 hours a day just updating HubSpot get a fully automated pipeline that logs itself — without replacing your existing setup or hiring an ops person."

**Cold copy CTA:** "10-minute walkthrough this week — I'll show you the exact setup in your stack before you commit."

**Scoring:**

| Dimension | Score | Reason |
|-----------|-------|--------|
| Specificity | 2 | "2 hours a day just updating HubSpot" — direct from evidence_quotes |
| Differentiation | 2 | "without replacing your existing setup" — directly counters enterprise/cost complaint |
| Reddit Language | 2 | "2 hours a day just updating HubSpot" is near-verbatim from quote |
| Outcome Clarity | 1 | States the outcome (automated pipeline) but only one measurable result |
| CTA Friction | 1 | "10-minute walkthrough" is specific and time-bound but CTA is medium friction |

**Total: 8 — PASSES validation.**

`offer_validation_score: 8, validation_warning: null, failed_validation: false`
