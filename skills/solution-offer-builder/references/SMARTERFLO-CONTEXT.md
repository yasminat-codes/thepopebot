## Offer Construction Formula

Every offer output from this skill must follow this formula exactly:

```
"We build [specific system name] that [specific outcome with metric] for [niche role] —
so you can [freedom or growth statement]."
```

The system name must be concrete (e.g. "AI candidate sourcing system", not "AI solution").
The outcome must contain a metric or timeframe where possible.
The freedom statement connects the system to what the client actually wants for their life or business.

**Worked examples:**

"We build an AI candidate sourcing system that surfaces 20 qualified candidates per week for recruitment agencies — so you can stop manual LinkedIn searching and focus on client relationships."

"We build an automated client reporting system that generates weekly reports in 2 minutes for marketing agencies — so you can handle 3x more clients without hiring."

"We build a prospect research pipeline that identifies ideal clients and writes personalised outreach for consulting firms — so you can fill your pipeline without a sales team."

"We build an AI content system that drafts, formats, and schedules 12 posts per month for B2B founders — so you can stay visible without content creation eating your week."

"We build an automated client onboarding system that takes new clients from signed to fully set up in 48 hours for agencies — so you can grow without your team getting stretched."

## Voice in Cold Copy — Yasmine as the Face

Cold copy written for Smarterflo should sound like Yasmine personally, not a corporate entity.

**First person rules:**

- Use: "I built..." "I help..." "I set this up for..." "I've worked with..."
- Do not use: "Our team specialises in..." "We offer..." "At Smarterflo, we believe..."
- Exception: The offer formula uses "We build..." for the positioning statement only — that is a brand positioning device, not conversational copy

**Voice pattern:**

- Reference the exact pain in the first line — not a paraphrase, the actual thing they said
- Bridge immediately to what Smarterflo built or built for someone similar
- One concrete result — a number, a timeframe, a before/after
- End with a low-friction CTA: "Open to a 15-min call to see if it's a fit?"
