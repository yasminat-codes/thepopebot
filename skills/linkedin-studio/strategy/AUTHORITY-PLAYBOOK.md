# Authority Playbook — Building Yasmine's LinkedIn Presence

> Stage-based authority building framework. Consumed by `ls:content-calendar`, `ls:content-pipeline`, `ls:performance-feedback-loop`.

**Current Stage: 1 — Unknown (starting fresh)**

---

## The 4 Stages

### Stage 1: Unknown (Current)
**Goal:** Get noticed. Build initial audience. Establish that you exist and know what you're talking about.

| Attribute | Guidance |
|---|---|
| Duration | 0-1,000 followers or first 3 months of consistent posting |
| Posting frequency | 4-5 posts/week (consistency > quality at this stage) |
| Pillar override | TL 40%, EDU 30%, SP 20%, CTA 10% (heavier on TL to get noticed) |
| Post formats | Text posts 80%, carousels 20% (text posts get more reach for new accounts) |
| Bold takes | Required. Safe content is invisible at this stage. |
| Engagement strategy | Comment on 10+ posts/day from bigger accounts in AI/tech space |
| Networking | Connect with 20+ relevant people/week (founders, ops leaders, AI builders) |
| Key metric | Impressions per post (are people seeing you?) |

**Stage 1 Weekly Arc:**

| Day | Pillar | Type | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| Monday | TL | Bold take / hot opinion | Start the week with energy, get engagement |
| Tuesday | EDU | How-to / framework | Provide value, build trust |
| Wednesday | SP or Story | Case study / personal story | Show proof or build connection |
| Thursday | TL or EDU | Industry insight or teaching | Reinforce expertise |
| Friday | CTA (optional) | Soft offer or engagement question | Convert interest, test offers |

**Transition signals → Stage 2:**
- Consistently getting 500+ impressions per post
- 3+ comments per post average
- 500+ followers
- Recognized by name in 2+ comment sections

---

### Stage 2: Recognized
**Goal:** People know your name and niche. Build engagement depth. Start converting followers into conversations.

| Attribute | Guidance |
|---|---|
| Duration | 1,000-5,000 followers or months 3-9 |
| Posting frequency | 4-5 posts/week |
| Pillar distribution | Return to standard: TL 35%, EDU 25%, SP 25%, CTA 15% |
| Post formats | Text 60%, carousels 30%, polls 10% |
| Engagement strategy | Comment on 5+ posts/day, respond to every comment on your posts |
| Content depth | Longer-form, more detailed, multi-part series welcome |
| Key metric | Engagement rate (are people interacting, not just seeing?) |

**Transition signals → Stage 3:**
- 3,000+ followers
- 5+ comments per post average
- Inbound DMs asking about services (organic, not prompted)
- Invited to collaborate or guest post
- Profile visits > 100/week

---

### Stage 3: Trusted
**Goal:** People trust your expertise. Inbound leads are regular. Focus on conversion and demonstrating depth.

| Attribute | Guidance |
|---|---|
| Duration | 5,000-20,000 followers or months 9-18 |
| Posting frequency | 3-4 posts/week (quality > quantity now) |
| Pillar distribution | TL 30%, EDU 20%, SP 30%, CTA 20% (more proof, more conversion) |
| Post formats | Mix freely — text, carousel, document, video |
| Engagement strategy | Selective engagement, focus on prospects and peers |
| Content depth | Case studies with full narratives, multi-slide deep dives |
| Key metric | Inbound leads per month |

**Transition signals → Stage 4:**
- 10,000+ followers
- Regular inbound leads (5+/month)
- Speaking invitations or podcast requests
- Content shared by industry peers
- Clients citing LinkedIn content as reason for reaching out

---

### Stage 4: Sought-After
**Goal:** You are the go-to person in your niche. Content drives business consistently. Selective about what you post.

| Attribute | Guidance |
|---|---|
| Duration | Ongoing |
| Posting frequency | 3-4 posts/week |
| Pillar distribution | TL 30%, EDU 15%, SP 30%, CTA 25% (heavy conversion) |
| Post formats | Whatever fits — you've earned format freedom |
| Key metric | Revenue attributed to LinkedIn |

---

## Stage-Specific Metrics That Matter

| Stage | Primary Metric | Secondary Metric | Vanity (Ignore) |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 Unknown | Impressions | Comments | Follower count |
| 2 Recognized | Engagement rate | Profile visits | Likes |
| 3 Trusted | Inbound DMs | Follower growth rate | Total impressions |
| 4 Sought-After | Revenue from LinkedIn | Inbound lead quality | All vanity metrics |

---

## Weekly Content Arc (Stage 1)

```
Mon ─── TL: Bold take, contrarian opinion, industry hot take
         Purpose: Pattern interrupt, get noticed

Tue ─── EDU: Framework, how-to, tool breakdown
         Purpose: Prove you know what you're talking about

Wed ─── SP/Story: Client result, personal story, behind-the-scenes
         Purpose: Build trust and connection

Thu ─── TL/EDU: Second thought leadership or teaching post
         Purpose: Reinforce expertise, stay visible

Fri ─── CTA (optional): Soft offer, engagement question, resource
         Purpose: Test offers, start conversations
```

This arc is a guideline, not a rigid rule. Adjust based on what is resonating.
